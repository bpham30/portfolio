A very simple animated background, that hopefully combines a complex animation with a reasonable file size.

Originally thought for MUGEN or such, it would need to be stretched to be used in higher resolutions, or cut and despeckled, however i reckon i have seen similar stuff behind puzzle games, so i thought somebody might find it useful.

It can also be used, on higher resolution backgrounds as something that is seen trough a window.

Also, i'm publishing the 3 floor elements and the wall alone in high resolution, The preview files are scaled down to 25%, thee files are 1920 pixel wide and, i'm confident, relatively small files. Regretfully, they aren't seamless tiles.

As usual i'm publishing a GIF sequence and a spritesheet for each element
